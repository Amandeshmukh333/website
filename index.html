FEATURE PRODUCTS

Fully Automatic Strapping Machine

Fully automatic box strapping machine uses polypropylene straps, a highly durable and cost-effective material, ensuring secure and reliable packaging. This makes it a go-to solution for industries where packaging speed and reliability are paramount.

Standard Top & Bottom Carton Sealing Machine

This machine automatically applies tape to both the top and bottom of the carton, ensuring a consistent, professional seal every time. It's perfect for businesses looking to speed up their packaging process without compromising the quality of their seal.

Automated Pet Strapping Tool

Strapping tool is designed for its PET straps, which are known for their high strength and durability. It enhances the strapping process by increasing labor productivity and eliminates usage of metal clips for sealing significantly improving both cost and speed.

Stretch Wrapping Machine

Automates the process of wrapping pallets or other large items with stretch film, ensuring uniform coverage and stabilization. This not only saves time but also reduces material waste, as the machine applies the right amount of film needed to securely wrap each load.
