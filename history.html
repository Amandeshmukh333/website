Our Legacy

From humble beginnings to industry leadership, explore the remarkable journey of ARCHANA ENTERPRISES in revolutionizing packaging solutions.

1995

Company Foundation

ARCHANA ENTERPRISES was established with a vision to revolutionize the packaging industry. Starting with a small team of dedicated professionals, we set out to create innovative solutions for businesses.

2000

Product Line Expansion

Introduced automated strapping machines and expanded our product portfolio. This marked a significant milestone in our journey towards becoming a comprehensive packaging solution provider.

2010

International Market Entry

Expanded operations to international markets and established global partnerships. Our commitment to quality and innovation helped us build strong relationships with clients worldwide.

2015

Innovation Award

Received industry recognition for innovative packaging solutions and customer service excellence. This achievement reinforced our position as a leader in the packaging industry.

2018

Digital Transformation

Implemented state-of-the-art digital solutions and automated manufacturing processes, setting new industry standards for efficiency and precision in packaging solutions.

2022

Sustainability Initiative

Launched eco-friendly packaging solutions and implemented green manufacturing practices, demonstrating our commitment to environmental sustainability and responsible business practices.
